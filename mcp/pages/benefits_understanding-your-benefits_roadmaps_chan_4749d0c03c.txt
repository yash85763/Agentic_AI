Changing your address
Whether you’re moving to a new home or just changing your phone number, be sure UC has up-to-date information on file so you don’t miss important benefits information or your W-2 form (if you elect to receive it in the mail).
Also, if you leave UC and decide to keep your UC Retirement Plan accumulations in the plan, you’ll want to make sure UC can reach you.
But don’t worry, changing your contact information is as easy as 1, 2, 3.
